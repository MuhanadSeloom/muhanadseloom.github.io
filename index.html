I design open-source, browser-based simulations that put students in decision-making roles within complex geopolitical scenarios. Simulations include UN debates on lethal autonomous weapons, crisis negotiations, intelligence failure analysis, and policy-memo exercises. I also integrate an "AI ledger" approach where students disclose and critically evaluate their use of generative AI â€” transforming it into a learning object rather than a policing problem.
